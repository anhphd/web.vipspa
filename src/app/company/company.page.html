VIPSPA
WELLNESS & SPA FOR EVERYONE

Located at Gaoming Zone, Foshan City, Guangdong Foshan Gaoming Yuehua Sanitary Ware Co.,Ltd. is a global sanitary ware manufacturer and exporter, selling to Europe, America and Asian countries. Established in 1995, Yuehua Sanitary Ware has set up sales network in the medium and large cities in China after more than ten years of hard working. In 2005 Yuehua obtained the self-importation and exportation rights.

Covering 30,000 m2 (with a under construction production base about 70,000m2), there are four major workshops: molding workshop, wood products workshop and manufacturer & assembling workshops for massage bathtubs, Swim spa and sauna room. With advanced technology and a team of talents, Yuehua provides the global buyers with "all-in-one service" including product design, molding, manufacture, sales and export trading.

Molding Workshop: There are different kinds of sophisticated facilities to ensure product quality, such as semi auto molding machine equipment from 4 meter to 12 meter, double location semi-automatic molding machine, semi-automatic edge trimmer, reinforced spray gun and water curtain dust removal, etc . Wood Product Workshop: There are automatic cutting machines, four side planer, sanding machines, semi-automatic edge trimmer, etc. Swimming Pool and Massage Bathtubs Workshop: There are semi-automatic molding machine equipment, double location semi automatic molding machine, semi auto edge trimmer and reinforced spray gun, etc.

Each workshop has standardized work flow from molding, strengthening, cutting, hole making, assembling and inspection to packing. Mature manufacturing system with strict management of each step ensures qualified products.

Brands: MEXDA, ANGESI and FACESPA are the three major brands of Yuehua. MEXDA was assessed as "Guangdong Famous Brand" in March, 2010.

Products
The main products are massage bathtubs, Steam Bathroom, Shower Enclosure, Bathroom Cabinet, sauna room and Swim spa, etc. Meanwhile, Yuehua also provide with OEM services.
